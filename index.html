Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises Toolbox

Supporting Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises for Sustainable Development

Welcome!

The MSME Toolbox consists of programmes supporting MSMEs, Governments, and individual entrepreneurs to build, support, fund, and scale businesses. This initiative is led by the UN ESCWA Technology Centre in partnership with UNCTAD, ESCAP, ECLAC, ECE, ECA, UN HABITAT, UNEP, UN OICT, the Royal Scientific Society (Jordan), the Higher Council for Science and Technology (Jordan), and Princess Sumaya University for Technology (Jordan).
